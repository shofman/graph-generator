Water Pipe puzzle
-----------------

Take the generated dungeon and create a series of pipes that are interconnected between the rooms.
Have certain rooms that require pipes to be switched / require pipes to be replaced with pieces from scattered through the dungeon
Once the water can flow from one part of the dungeon to the next, the goal is achieved

Text Rotation puzzle
---------------------
Sample puzzle:
A 1 S 3 5 T 
R 2 8 Z K 9

Perform a series of rotations on either middle section, end section or beginning section

E.g. rotation counterclockwise on first section would be:

1 2 S 3 5 T 
A R 8 Z K 9

Perform a series of these steps, and then force the user to figure out the previous steps
